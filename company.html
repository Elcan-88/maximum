About us

Maximum is dedicated to manufacturing and delivering lubricants and autochemicals on par or exceeding international standards and OEM requirements. We conduct business responsibly and in a way that places weight on following international regulations and business ethics.

Maximum uses state-of-the-art facilities worldwide to manufacture its lubricants and auto-chemicals. In manufacturing only supreme base stock and additives are used, allowing our engineers to formulate products that meet or exceed international standards.

Following delivery support and development of new products also receives significant attention, with our flexible approach allowing us to use feedback from support operations to develop new, more specific, and more effective products. Our dedicated logistics and fulfilment teams work diligently to deliver goods to customers all over the world in the most parsimonious and timely manner.

Our mission

We continue to build strong trade routes to ensure that we can transport our products to global markets in a fast and inexpensive way. In all aspects of management, we assign an utmost importance to quality control and quality assurance for customers.

In fact, we have a dedicated, internationally accredited laboratory in house, to ensure that what we make is on par with international standards and requirements of OEMs.

A few years ago, our insights into the lubricants and auto-chemicals market led us to the discovery that, although most products share the same raw materials and formulations, most products on the consumer market are massively overpriced. Maximum was borne from the desire to tackle this artificial inflation to offer professional products at fair prices.

Since then, we have built strong relations with dedicated distributors in various markets. We are proud to have a team who truly believe in the product and what it stands for.

Our vision

We also aim to grow our reach into new markets through adding more companies to our esteemed list of partners. We are looking for distributors who truly believe in the brand, and are ready to represent our product on their market in a sincere and dynamic way.

We aim to develop new products based on feedback and research on the products that we manufacture and deliver currently. We aim to achieve better results with our oil and maintenance fluids particularly through ensuring that our products maintain performance characteristics even under the most demanding conditions.
