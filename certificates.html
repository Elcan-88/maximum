Responsibility

Certain aspects of our business transcend what is and can be written on paper.

Maximum aims to be a responsible partner by adapting a conscious approach to civil rights, environmental protection laws, workplace safety rules, and employee protection in manufacturing and delivering a variety of lubricants and service fluids.

In manufacturing we only use 100% virgin materials, steering clear of any and all recycled products. In addition, all products which are sources are procured from international companies with a strong reputation for reliability and quality. Nonetheless, the materials accepted for use as raw materials are first tested down to 10ppm accuracy for the relevant parameters to ensure the quality. We benchmark the manufacturing process by randomly testing each batch at least twice before it reaches our clients..

In all out business dealings we adhere to international business regulations and business ethics. We hold integrity as a principle of utmost importance and encourage its practive at all levels of management within the company.
