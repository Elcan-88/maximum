Reasons to
choose Maximum

First and foremost, we offer laboratory analyses free of charge both on the product that we sell, and the field samples fed back to us from our suppliers and contractors. Our in-house lab is internationally certified, and can provide virtually all tests associated with lubricants and auto-chemicals. Rest assured that all raw materials procured and all products delivered are subject to stringent internal benchmarking rules that we do not compromise. We attach the utmost importance to quality and requirements of our customers.

Second, we can deliver our products in all packing forms. Ranging from the smallest 250ml for brake fluid to 21,000 L in a flexi-tank container. Not only can we fill different volumes, we also have a dedicated team that can plan the route and deliver your goods under conditions required. We are capable of making global deliveries.

Third, we hold integrity as a principle of import in all our dealings. With us, there are no unpleasant surprises. We support our customers to the best of our ability, and when we are unable to do so we find someone who can. We are the company that goes the extra mile, and we are proud of this approach.
